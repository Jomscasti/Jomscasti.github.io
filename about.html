Hello, I'm Jomari Castillo

I'm from Paciano Rizal, Calamba Laguna — an optimistic and motivated IT professional with a passion for design, technology, and digital innovation. I thrive in dynamic environments and am driven by the belief that creativity, collaboration, and continuous growth are key to success.

As a proud Microsoft Youth Ambassador and one of the pioneering graduates of MYA 2022, I now serve as a MYA Fellow, mentoring aspiring tech leaders across the Philippines. I study Bachelor of Science in Information Technology at the Polytechnic University of the Philippines – Sto. Tomas, Batangas.

I currently serve as the Graphic Design Lead and Social Media Manager for the Google Developer Student Clubs. I am also the Vice President for External Affairs of the Computer Society and the Chairperson of Sentinel Esports.
